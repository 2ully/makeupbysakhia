About Me

Hi, I'm Skhia

I'm a professional makeup artist with over 8 years of experience transforming looks for weddings, editorial shoots, and special events. Based locally and available for travel.

My philosophy is simple: makeup should enhance who you already are. I work with your features, skin tone, and personal style to create a look that feels authentically you — whether that's a barely-there glow or full editorial drama.

Certified in advanced bridal techniques and airbrush application, I stay current with industry trends while never losing sight of timeless beauty.
